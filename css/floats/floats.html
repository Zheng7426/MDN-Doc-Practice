When will the moon be full and bright? Raising my wineglass, I ask the blue sky. I know not what year it is tonight, In the far-away heaven palace. I wish to return home with fair wind, For fear I cannot bear the freezing cold, So high in the marble towers and jade houses. I dance with my lonely shadow wild, As if I were not in the human world.
